Bootstrap Grid: The 12-Column System

Bootstrap divides every row into 12 equal units. A column class like col-md-4 means "take up 4 of those 12 units" — i.e. one third of the row. Columns that don't add up to 12 leave empty space; columns that exceed 12 wrap to the next line.
